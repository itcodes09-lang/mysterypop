PROJECT PROPOSAL: MysteryPop - A Premium Blind Box & Collectibles Platform

Project Description

MysteryPop is a Node.js-based e-commerce platform designed for the booming blind box and designer toy industry. Unlike traditional retail, MysteryPop introduces a gamified shopping experience where users purchase sealed "mystery boxes" containing random, high-value collectibles, anime figures, or tech gadgets. The system allows users to buy individual boxes or entire "cases," open boxes virtually via a dynamic live-unboxing interface, and trade duplicates with other users. The backend combines a robust, event-driven Node.js environment with strict probability engines to ensure fair drops, secure checkout, and lightning-fast real-time inventory updates.
